Ravah ingests deployment metadata, logs, incidents, and infrastructure signals.

- **Normalization layer**: standardizes telemetry across providers.
- **Rules layer**: deterministic checks for known risk patterns.
- **Reasoning layer**: AI-assisted synthesis with evidence references.

Outputs include pipeline risk, RCA timelines, and suggested remediation guardrails.
