## Why DORA metrics need context

Teams tracking DORA metrics often collect raw numbers without the context needed to improve them. Ravah.ai pairs pipeline events with incident timelines so you can see *why* metrics move, not just *that* they move.

## Practical steps

- Connect CI/CD data to on-call timelines.
- Highlight the evidence behind every change failure.
- Track improvements tied to real fixes, not cosmetic changes.
